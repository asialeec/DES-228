Takashi Murakami

Murakami inspires me to dream big and actually let your imagination run wild, regardless of the vulgarity and obsenity. He redefines how even the smallest of details can make or add to the bigger picture.



Pharrell Williams

Pharrell Williams is a prime example of how you don't need to confine oneself to just one medium. He inspires me to dip into whatever art forms that pique my interest as he's made an impact in every one he's dabbled in.
